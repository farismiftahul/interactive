Prepositions of Time: In / On / At

1. We watch different types of movies ___ different days.
on
2. Come to our club ___ Mondays.
on
3. ___ Mondays, we watch only horror and adventure movies.
On
4. ___ the morning, we watch two horror movies.
In
5. We enjoy meeting our club members ___ lunch.
at
6. ___ the afternoon, we watch an adventure movie.
In
7. After the movie, we have a coffee break ___ 4 pm.
at
8. Visit our club ___ Tuesdays, then.
on
9. ___ the evening, there is a comedy movie dinner.
In
10. It is ___ 6 pm.
at
