Wonder Woman — Movie Review Game (Writing Prep)

This interactive page guides students from reading comprehension to planning and writing their own movie review.

How to use: read the passage on the left, complete the mini-games, then write your review in the box.

Reading Passage

Wonder Woman

My review is about the new DC movie Wonder Woman. It's a fantasy-action movie. Wonder Woman (or Diana) is the princess of the Amazons, and she lives on an island. Diana is a real warrior, and she leaves the island with a soldier because she wants to stop the world war.

The director of the film is Petty Jenkins, and I think he is very successful. There are also many stars in the movie. Gal Gadot and Chris Pane are some of the stars in the movie. My favourite character in the movie is Diana / Wonder Woman. She is strong and beautiful. The movie is also great because there is a lot of action in it.

Go and see the movie at the weekend. It's a five-star movie.

Write your own short review

Use the passage as a model. Try to include: title, two-sentence summary, one opinion sentence, and a recommendation + 1–5 star rating.

Teacher rubric (quick):
  • Title: present and clear (2 pts)
  • Summary: 1–2 sentences that explain plot (4 pts)
  • Opinion: clear opinion sentence with reason (3 pts)
  • Recommendation & rating: present (1 pt)

Hints & Language Frames

Opening / Title: "Wonder Woman — A short review"

Summary frame: "The movie is about... She/He leaves the island because..."

Opinion frame: "I think the movie is... because..."

Recommendation: "I recommend this movie because..."

Teacher tools

Printable peer-review checklist (copy for students):

  1. Is there a clear title?
  2. Does the summary explain the plot in 1–2 sentences?
  3. Is the opinion sentence supported by a reason?
  4. Is there a recommendation and a star rating?
