Present Tenses

Present Simple & Present Continuous — interactive lesson

Present Simple: Meaning

We use the present simple for:

  • habits and routines
  • facts and general truths
  • things that are always or usually true

Click the verbs:

I wake up at 6.
She works in a bank.
Water freezes at 0°C.

Present Simple: Affirmative

I / you / we / they + base verb

he / she / it + verb + -s / -es

I play → He plays

We go → She goes

Present Simple: Negative

do not (don't) / does not (doesn't) + base verb

I don't like coffee.

She doesn't eat meat.

Present Simple: Questions

Do / Does + subject + base verb

Do you live here?

Does she play tennis?

Present Continuous: Meaning

We use the present continuous for:

  • actions happening now
  • temporary situations
  • things happening around this time

Present Continuous: Form

am / is / are + verb + -ing

I am studying English.

She is watching TV.

They are playing football.

Present Continuous: Use

Common time expressions:

now • at the moment • right now • today

Listen! The baby is crying.

I am reading a good book this week.

Present Simple vs Present Continuous

  • Present simple → habits, facts
  • Present continuous → happening now / temporary

I work in a hospital. (permanent)

I am working late today. (temporary)
