Interactive grammar lesson: meaning • form • use

What does the present simple mean?

We use the present simple to talk about things that are:

  • true in general
  • repeated or regular
  • part of everyday life

When do we use it?

  • Habits and routines
  • Facts and scientific truths
  • Schedules and timetables
